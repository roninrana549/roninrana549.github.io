What is Bushido

Bushido is not just a brand, Bushido is a culture. Inspired by the code of honour of Samurais from Feudal Japan, Bushido aims to correlate this culture with our own. Providing premium products ethically manufactured in Nepal we give you a collection of Exclusive products.



Tees



Our tshirts are manufactured and designed ethically and purely in Nepal. It is made from Terry cotton which gives a very premium feel to the fabric. The designs are exclusive to our own brands which you will not find anywhere else in the Market



Hoodies



Our hoodies are solely manufactured in Nepal with exclusive designs and limited collections every season with the same premium feel to the fabric as our tees.
